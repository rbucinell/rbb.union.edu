Satisfactory completion of this course should demonstrate that students have:


    1. The ability to identify and formulate elementary level engineering problems related to static structures, in conceptual form as well as in terms of mathematical and physical models;
    2. The ability to apply the basic principles of mechanics to the analysis of static structures;
    3. The ability to understand and effectively communicate the fundamentals of static analysis and synthesis;
    4. The ability to effectively generalize the basic axioms of statics, and to extrapolate those concepts to structures not necessarily covered specifically in the course presented;
    5. Gain a physical understanding of the mechanics of static structures.
