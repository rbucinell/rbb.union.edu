Satisfactory completion of this course should demonstrate that students have:

 

  1. Have enough of an understanding of the field of engineering to decide the appropriate discipline to study.
  2. An introductory familiarity to (tools (Excel, Matlab, SolidWorks, etc) used in the engineering profession.
  3. An introduction to the methods engineers use to communicate their ideas.
  4. An understanding of the responsibilities of an engineer to society and the profession.
  5. An ability to form and effectively work in a team environment.
  6. An understanding of the innovation process and how engineering design fits into this process.
  7. A fundamental understanding of units and significant figures.
